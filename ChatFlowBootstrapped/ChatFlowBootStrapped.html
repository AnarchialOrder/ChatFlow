        Camera Connection Issues

        Your Camera uses WiFi to broadcast it's live feed to both the Touchscreen Controller and your App/Subscriber Portal.
        Due to this, it will depend on the strength of your home network's WiFi signal.
        WiFi strength depends on the distance of the two devices and any interference that may be present.
        The distance you should keep your camera,(or Camera Power Brick if you have the xCam), from the Touchscreen Controller and your Modem or Router should be no more than 35 feet.
        Interference can be caused by thick surfaces (e.g., concrete floors and walls) and other household electronics, such as baby monitors, cordless phones, microwave ovens, refrigerators and Bluetooth-connected devices. Also, try to avoid crowded areas, such as inside or behind furniture, like an entertainment console.
        What I would recommend is that you make sure that the Touchscreen Controller and the Modem or Router for your Internet connection both have an uninterrupted line of sight to your Camera (or Camera Power Brick, if you have the xCam.)
        If there is interference, I will have to recommend that you move the modem and Touchscreen Controller closer to strengthen the WiFi signal for the camera.

        If it helps, here is an article that details how to improve your wifi. The principles outlined there apply to your Secure devices as well: https://www.xfinity.com/support/internet/improve-your-wireless-home-network/

    Arming and Disarming
    • +

      Modes
      You can arm your XFINITY Home - Secure system in three modes: Arm Away, Arm Stay, and Arm Night.

      Arm Away gives you time to leave the house (Exit Delay) and then activates your system, including the Motion Sensors. When you come home, you have time to disarm the system (Entry Delay) so you don't trigger an alarm. If you activate Arm Away and no one leaves the house using a monitored door, the system automatically switches to Arm Stay mode.

      Arm Stay is for when you are at home and awake. Security features are activated as in Arm Away, but Motion Sensors are not (so you don't trip the alarm while you're in the house).

      Arm Night is for when you go to sleep. The system is activated as in Arm Away and Arm Stay, but with no Entry Delay when you disarm the system. Motion Sensors can be activated with or without an Exit Delay, depending on your setup. Typically, Motion Sensors are activated immediately in areas where you are not going to be, while Motion Sensors are delayed in places you'll pass through on your way to your bedroom.

    • +

      Exit Delays
      EXIT DELAYS:

      ARM AWAY: If you open and shut an Entry/Exit door during the Exit Delay and then re-enter the house, the Exit Delay restarts at 120 seconds. It will only do this one time. If an Entry/Exit door is left open at the end of Exit Delay, the Entry Delay immediately starts and, if the system is not disarmed, an alarm will sound.

      ARM STAY: If you open and shut an Entry or Exit door during the Exit Delay and then re-enter the premises, the Exit Delay restarts. It will only do this one time. If an Entry or Exit door is left open at the end of Exit Delay, the Entry Delay immediately starts and, if the system is not disarmed, an alarm will sound.

      If you shut an Entry or Exit door during the Exit Delay and then re-enter the premises, the Exit Delay restarts. It will only do this one time.

      There is no entry delay for the Arm Night mode, the system will sound the siren.

      How to arm
      HOW TO ARM:
      Wireless Keypads

      =================

      Press the Arm state you want the system in, and then your four digit pin.


      Disarm System

      ==============

      Open an Entry/Exit door.
      Listen for a tone indicating that the Entry Delay has started.
      Enter your Master Keypad Code or Secondary Keypad Code before the Entry Delay period ends.

      SMC is the only one where you enter in the code first, and then the arm state.

      ARM/DISARM FROM THE TOUCHSCREEN CONTROLLER:
      Tap the Security shield.
      On the Select Arm State screen, Away mode is already selected. To activate another mode, tap the icon for Stay or Night.
      Enter your keypad code. The Exit Delay begins.
      You have until the Exit Delay is over to exit the building. An entry/exit door must open and close during this period.
      Change Your Mind? To cancel arming, tap Cancel, and then enter your keypad code.
      Enter your keypad code before the Entry Delay ends. The system is disarmed.
